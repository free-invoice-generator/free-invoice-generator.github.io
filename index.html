What is an Invoice?

An invoice is a document issued by a business to a client, detailing services or products provided and requesting payment. It includes specifics like item descriptions, quantities, prices, and payment terms. A free invoice generator simplifies this process, allowing businesses to create professional invoices using a free invoice template quickly and efficiently.
